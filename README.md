**Power BI Dashboard Projects**

### Project 1 – Call Center Performance Analysis Dashboard

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze call center operations, monitor service quality, and evaluate customer satisfaction. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making and operational efficiency improvements.

**Key analyses and KPIs included:**

* Total number of customer inquiries, answered calls, and response rate analysis
* Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) metrics and average rating evaluation
* Detailed analysis of inquiries by category, topic, and region
* Employee performance tracking and productivity measurement
* Weekly and monthly trends in customer inquiries and service demand
* Customer segmentation and demographic analysis based on age groups
* Identification of the most common customer issues and service bottlenecks
* Interactive KPI monitoring and performance reporting

### Project 2 – CRM & Sales Performance Dashboard

Designed and developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, track revenue growth, and monitor sales pipeline effectiveness using CRM data. The dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate business performance and identify growth opportunities.

**Key analyses and KPIs included:**

* Overall sales performance, revenue tracking, and key business metrics
* Distribution and analysis of Won, Lost, and Open opportunities
* Sales funnel analysis and opportunity conversion monitoring
* Monthly trends in opportunities, sales volume, and revenue generation
* Regional sales performance comparison and market analysis
* Performance evaluation of sales representatives and sales managers
* Customer, industry, and product-level sales analysis
* Identification of top-performing customers, products, and market segments
* Interactive reporting to support strategic decision-making and business growth initiatives
